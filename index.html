2022: A Year of Revelations

⚠ CONTENT WARNINGS AHEAD! ⚠
Proceed at your own discretion!

"2022: A Year of Revelations", is a cumulative video project created by me, Unknown68D. In early 2022, I discovered that for the majority of my life, I've suffered from a mental illness known as OCD (Obsessive Compulsive Disorder). It's a debilitating condition in which sufferers are bombarded with obsessions, the contents of intrusive, unwanted thoughts. These obsessions are followed by compulsions, (rituals, whether mental or physical) which attempt to relieve the negative emotions and sensations that arise from the obsessions.

I spent the rest of the year in therapy, where I finally began truly understand myself and how my mind works. As I learned more about the condition and listened to stories from my fellow OCD sufferers, the spark that is the idea for this video began to ignite. From there, I sought to share my story with the world to help them, the internet generation and all individuals with their mental health.

Clocking in at a little over 7 hours, I present a culmination of all my thoughts, grievances and opinions on the current state of the world as of 2022, along with how the internet has influenced it and what we can do to navigate our current society among other things both previously mentioned and more!

If you're ready for it, sit back, strap in and enjoy the ride!

OCD's Taboo Territory

⚠ CONTENT WARNINGS AHEAD! ⚠
Proceed at your own discretion!

"OCD's Taboo Territory" is a spiritual successor to "2022: A Year of Revelations", my first ever video essay where I discussed my OCD diagnosis for the first time. This video's central focus is on the taboo side of this debilitating disorder, arguing that it is one of the most stigmatized and misunderstood mental illnesses in modern-day society, whose obstacles extend beyond mere ignorance and into active pushback territory, meaning there's a deliberate effort and incentivization towards not understanding it, thus further stigmatizing it.

I'm here to help make a breakthrough in the mental health landscape, with one of my personal long-term goals being to bridge the gap between OCD-awareness and the internet generation; to have more informative, compassionate conversations surrounding sensitive topics, as well as dismantling stigma wherever possible. Additionally, I discuss topics of purity culture and sex-positivity through an intersectional approach, as both of these topics contains attitudes, paradigms and frameworks which are useful to dissect to gain key insights which have rich intersectionality with OCD & enhances understanding of the societal influences at play. I argue that the former is a negative one, & that the latter is a positive one. In other words, a new path forward for the betterment of society.

I hope you enjoy, plus receive the validation, education & healing you deserve!

SMBX: The Epic Quest (A Super Mario Bros. X Episode)

In this Super Mario Bros. X (A Super Mario fangame) episode, join Mario, Luigi, Toad & Toadette as you partake in this traditional, yet benevolent epic. Essentially, it's an episode that takes great inspiration from the NSMB series in terms of world layout and level progression, heavily incorporating elements from many classic 2D Mario games you all know & love! It features the classic 8 world themes, as well as a secret 9th world!

In terms of the story, it's about what you expect from many of the mainline Mario titles out there, in which you rescue Princess Peach. However, Bowser has more of a... scientific... motive for kidnapping Peach this time around. You'll meet various friends, learn more about the history of the Mushroom Kingdom & embark on numerous challenges as you traverse Bowser's Darklands.

With over 100 unique levels, 90 stars to collect, 20 secret exits & a variety of boss fights both nostalgic & innovative, it will certainly be an epic to remember!

I have been a long-time player of SMBX for many years to date, and I've been working on this episode on and off for as long as I can remember. This has been BY FAR my most pressing passion project to date, spanning almost an entire decade! After rekindling my motivation for Nintendo-level creating after 2-3 years of inactivity in between, I set out to finish what I've started. I've been intensively working on completing this project ever since! Now, I am both proud & ecstatic to finally release this project to both the SMBX community & my online community!

I hope that you will all enjoy everything that this episode has to offer! I certainly enjoyed pouring my heart & soul into every last object placed. I wouldn't change this journey for the world, as it ignited a passion for creating & game design within me that I never would have obtained otherwise.

Thank you to everyone who has contributed, whether directly or indirectly, to this project's completion! Enjoy embarking on your epic quest!

Burnout 3: Tour De L'Unknown

“Burnout 3: Tour De L'Unknown” is a fan-made world tour (aka campaign) in the beloved hit classic: Burnout 3: Takedown. This project is intended for players who are already familiar with the base game and completed it 100%.

This world tour has you competing in various new events across the 3 regions and 18 tracks you know & love! Some events are unique takes on classic events such as Race, Road Rage, Burning Lap and Face-Off, while others are completely new event types, such as Rage-Off, Marked Man & No-Crash Dash! Rules for all of these events are provided in the links above.

This campaign will push your driving skills to the limits on the Burnout road, encouraging you to take even MORE risks that you never thought possible, as well as changes in the way you approach events. With 8 events per track (2 in both directions TIMES TWO for mirror mode), and 18 tracks PLUS 4 Grand Prix events at the end of it all, this is the burner's ultimate test of speed, skill, aggression & tactical maneuvers!

If you think you've got what it takes to conquer them all, check out the links above to get started! This has been a public service announcement brought to you by 68D Radio!
